🌸 Week of July 13, 2026

He Goes Before You

📖 Scripture

"The Lord Himself goes before you and will be with you; He will never leave you nor forsake you. Do not be afraid; do not be discouraged."

Deuteronomy 31:8

💜 Weekly Encouragement

One of the greatest comforts we have as believers is knowing that we never walk alone.

Before we take our first step, God has already gone before us.

He already knows the road ahead. He already sees every turn, every blessing, every challenge, and every opportunity waiting for us.

Sometimes we become afraid because we cannot see what tomorrow holds. But God never asked us to trust tomorrow. He asked us to trust Him.

If He goes before us, then we never have to fear the unknown.

Walk confidently this week—not because you know what tomorrow holds, but because you know the One who does.

🙏 Prayer

Heavenly Father, thank You for always going before me. When I cannot see the path ahead, remind me that You already know every step. Replace my fear with faith, my worry with peace, and my uncertainty with confidence in Your promises. Help me trust You more each day and remember that I never walk alone because You are always with me. In Jesus' precious name, Amen.

🌷 Weekly Reflection:
Where is God asking me to trust Him instead of my own understanding this week?

Love,
🦋 Amber

💜 Remember this week: The Lord goes before you. He will never leave you nor forsake you.

You are loved. You are chosen. You are worthy. You are never alone.

His Mercies Are New Every Morning

📖 Scripture

May my heart remain soft, grateful, and willing to follow wherever You lead. In Jesus' name, Amen.

When God Changes the Route

📖 Scripture

"Trust in the Lord with all your heart and lean not on your own understanding."

Proverbs 3:5

💜 Encouragement

Have you ever had plans that suddenly changed?

Maybe a door closed. Maybe life took an unexpected turn. Maybe God led you somewhere you never planned to go.

I've learned that God's detours are never mistakes.

Sometimes He protects us from roads we cannot see. Sometimes He prepares us before opening the next door. Sometimes He simply wants us to trust Him more deeply.

If your path looks different than you expected today, don't lose heart.

The God who created the road also knows where it leads.

Trust His direction more than your own map.

Lead me where You want me to go, and help me follow You with confidence, knowing that You never make mistakes. In Jesus' name, Amen.

🌸 July 1, 2026

One Yes Can Change Everything

📖 Scripture

"Trust in the Lord with all your heart and lean not on your own understanding; in all your ways submit to Him, and He will make your paths straight."

Proverbs 3:5–6

💜 Encouragement

Have you ever noticed that God rarely shows us the entire journey?

Instead, He asks for one step. One act of faith. One "yes."

Looking back, I can see that the biggest changes in my life did not happen all at once. They happened each time I trusted God enough to take the next step He placed before me.

That is what being God's Yes Girl means to me. Not being fearless. Not having every answer. Simply believing that His direction is always better than my own.

Maybe today your "yes" looks like forgiving someone. Maybe it is praying when you do not feel like it. Maybe it is beginning something you have been afraid to start.

Whatever it is, remember this: God does not ask us to carry tomorrow. He simply asks us to trust Him today.

One yes can change everything.

🙏 Prayer

Father, teach me to trust You with my whole heart. Help me lay down my plans whenever they compete with Yours. Give me the courage to say yes to whatever You ask, knowing that You are faithful in every step. May my life always point others to You. In Jesus' name, Amen.

Love,
🦋 Amber

💜 Remember today: You are loved. You are chosen. You are worthy. You are never alone.

🌸 June 30, 2026

The Beginning of Something Beautiful

📖 Scripture

"Being confident of this, that He who began a good work in you will carry it on to completion until the day of Christ Jesus."

Philippians 1:6

💜 Encouragement

Today marks the beginning of something special.

Not because a website was finished. Not because another project was completed. But because I said "yes" to the little step God placed in front of me.

Sometimes we wait for the perfect moment before we begin. God often asks us to begin first, trusting that He will shape the journey along the way.

Today I am choosing to believe that every small act of obedience matters. Every prayer, every encouraging word, every step of faith can become part of something much bigger than I can see today.

If you are reading this, maybe God is inviting you to take one small step too. You do not have to know the whole path. Just trust the One who does.

🙏 Prayer

Father, thank You for new beginnings. Help me remember that You are the Author of every good thing You call me to do. Give me courage to keep saying yes, even when I do not know what tomorrow holds. Use this journal to encourage hearts, point people toward You, and remind us all that Your plans are always greater than our own. In Jesus' name, Amen.

Love,
🦋 Amber
